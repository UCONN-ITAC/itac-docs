# Don't Pay Sales Tax on Utility Purchases

In Connecticut (and other states, qualification must be researched), manufacturers who use at least 75% of their utility consumption for manufacturing or fabrication are able to purchase their electricity tax free. More specific information can be found [here](https://ics-tax.com/utility-sales-tax-exemption/connecticut-utility-sales-tax-exemption/#:~:text=Connecticut%20offers%20a%20sales%20tax,to%20clearly%20measure%20qualifying%20consumption.).

## Identifying Where the Recommendation is Applicable

It is a safe bet that any manufacturer who is requesting an energy assessment from ITAC is using >75% of their utility consumption for qualifying activities, what is important to identify before writing this recommendation is if they have already claimed this exemption.
